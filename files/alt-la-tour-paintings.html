    Description

    Maurice Quentin de La Tour was a French painter who worked primarily with pastels in the Rococo style. Among his most famous subjects were Voltaire, Rousseau, Louis XV and the Madame de Pompadour. Wikipedia
    Born: September 5, 1704, Saint-Quentin, France
    Died: February 17, 1788 (age 83 years), Saint-Quentin, France
    Period: Rococo
