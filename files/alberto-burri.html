    About

    Description

    Alberto Burri was an Italian visual artist, painter, sculptor, and physician based in Citt di Castello. He is associated with the matterism of the European informal art movement and described his style as a polymaterialist. Wikipedia
    Born: March 12, 1915, Citt di Castello, Italy
    Died: February 13, 1995 (age 79 years), Nice, France
    Spouse: Minsa Craig (m. 19551995)
    Movement: Fluxus, Nouveau ralisme, Arte Povera
    Parents: Carolina Torreggiani, Pietro Burri
    Notable work: Cretto di Burri
