      Description

      Description

      Ren Franois Ghislain Magritte was a Belgian surrealist artist. He became well known for creating a number of witty and thought-provoking images. Often depicting ordinary objects in an unusual context, his work is known for challenging observers' preconditioned perceptions of reality. Wikipedia
      Born: November 21, 1898, Lessines, Belgium
      Died: August 15, 1967, Schaerbeek, Schaerbeek, Belgium
      On view: The Museum of Modern Art, The Art Institute of Chicago, MORE
      Periods: Surrealism, Art Deco, Cubism, Post-Impressionism, Modern art, Dada
      Full name: Ren Franois Ghislain Magritte
      Influenced by: Andr Breton, Sigmund Freud, Jack Boynton
