      Brendan James Fraser is an American and Canadian actor. He is best known for playing Rick O'Connell in The Mummy trilogy, as well as for leading roles in comedy and fantasy films including Encino Man, George of the Jungle, Bedazzled, Looney Tunes: Back in Action and Journey to the Center of the Earth.
