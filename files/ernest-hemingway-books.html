      Ernest Miller Hemingway was an American novelist, short-story writer, journalist, and sportsman. His economical and understated stylewhich he termed the iceberg theoryhad a strong influence on 20th-century fiction, while his adventurous lifestyle and his public image brought him admiration from later generations.
