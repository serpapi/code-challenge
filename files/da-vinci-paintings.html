    Leonardo da Vinci

    Polymath

    Description

    Description

    Leonardo da Vinci was an Italian polymath of the High Renaissance who was active as a painter, draughtsman, theorist, engineer, scientist, sculptor and architect. Wikipedia
    Born: April 15, 1452, Anchiano, Italy
    Died: May 2, 1519, Chteau du Clos Luc, Amboise, France
    On view: Louvre Museum, Royal Collection Trust, MORE
    Periods: High Renaissance, Early renaissance, Renaissance, Italian Renaissance, Florentine painting
    Structures: Leonardo's Rivellino
    Series: Madonna of the Yarnwinder, Leda and the Swan
    Fields: Painting, Geometry, Anatomy
