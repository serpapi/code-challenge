      Born: July 26, 1928, Manhattan, New York, United States
      Died: March 7, 1999, Childwickbury, United Kingdom
      Spouse: Christiane Kubrick (m. 1958–1999), Ruth Sobotka (m. 1955–1957), Toba Metz (m. 1948–1951)
