        Description

        Pixar Animation Studios, commonly known as just Pixar, is an American computer animation studio known for its critically and commercially successful computer animated feature films. It is based in Emeryville, California, and is a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios owned by The Walt Disney Company. Wikipedia
        Founded: February 3, 1986, Richmond, California, United States
        Headquarters: Emeryville, California, United States
        President: Jim Morris
        Acquisition date: 2006
        Parent organizations: The Walt Disney Company, Walt Disney Studios
        Founders: John Lasseter, Steve Jobs, Edwin Catmull, Alvy Ray Smith, Alexander Schure
