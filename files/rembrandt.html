      Description

      Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn was a Dutch draughtsman, painter, and printmaker. An innovative and prolific master in three media, he is generally considered one of the greatest visual artists in the history of art and the most important in Dutch art history. Wikipedia
      Born: July 15, 1606, Leiden, Netherlands
      Died: October 4, 1669, Amsterdam, Netherlands
      On view: Rijksmuseum, National Gallery of Art, MORE
      Periods: Baroque, Dutch Golden Age, Baroque painting
      Full name: Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn
      Nationality: Dutch
      Spouse: Saskia van Uylenburgh (m. 16341642)
