    Edgar Degas
    French artist

    Description

    Edgar Degas was a French artist famous for his pastel drawings and oil paintings of ballerinas. Degas also produced bronze sculptures, prints, and drawings. Degas is especially identified with the subject of dance; more than half of his works depict dancers. Wikipedia
    Born: July 19, 1834, Paris, France
    Died: September 27, 1917, Paris, France
    On view: National Gallery of Art, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, MORE
    Periods: Impressionism, Modern art, Realism, Neoclassicism
    Known for: Painting, sculpture, drawing
    Education: University of Paris, Lyce Louis-le-Grand, National School of Fine Arts
    Quotes
    Art is not what you see, but what you make others see.
    Painting is easy when you don't know how, but very difficult when you do.
    What a delightful thing is the conversation of specialists! One understands absolutely nothing and it's charming.
