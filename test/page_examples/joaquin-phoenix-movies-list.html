      Joaquin Phoenix
      Actor

      Description

      Joaquin Rafael Phoenix is an American actor, producer, and activist, who has received several accolades, including a Grammy Award, a Golden Globe Award, and nominations for three Academy Awards. Phoenix started acting in television series with his brother River Phoenix and sister Summer Phoenix. Wikipedia
      Born: October 28, 1974 (age 44 years), San Juan, Puerto Rico
      Height: 1.73 m
      TV shows: The Fall Guy, Seven Brides for Seven Brothers, MORE
      Awards: Golden Globe Awards, Grammy Awards
      Siblings: River Phoenix, Rain Phoenix, Summer Phoenix, Jodean Bottom, Liberty Phoenix
